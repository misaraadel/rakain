Our architectural practice is grounded in a deep understanding of cultural context and responds to the needs of contemporary life through a clear design approach that seeks to create long-term architectural value

Founded in 2017 as Rowshan, the company continued in 2025 under the name Rakain with a more defined identity and a more consistent architectural approach. Each project is seen as an opportunity to reinterpret tradition through contemporary design, efficient spatial planning, and a balanced architectural language.

EN | Abdulmajid & Nawaf

Rakain was established through a professional partnership between architects AbdulMajeed AlAwwad and Nawaf AlDahash, shaped by a shared academic background and a professional journey that formed a unified architectural vision. Today, they lead the company with a clear focus on design quality, technical excellence, strengthening local architectural identity, and creating architectural spaces of long-term value that contribute meaningfully to the built environment.

Vision

To shape architecture that endures in value and meaning across cultures and contexts.

Mission

To design architecture that endures, grounded in deep cultural understanding, clear architectural thinking, and a strong commitment to engineering standards, quality, and design excellence.

Architectural Design

We provide comprehensive architectural solutions starting from feasibility studies and concept development, all the way to detailed execution plans. Our goal is to create buildings that exceed functional expectations.

Interior Design

We create stunning interior spaces that blend aesthetics with functionality, ensuring every detail reflects your vision and lifestyle.

Landscape

Transform outdoor spaces into beautiful, sustainable environments that complement architectural designs and enhance quality of life.

Project Management

Expert project management ensuring timely delivery, budget control, and quality standards throughout the construction process.
